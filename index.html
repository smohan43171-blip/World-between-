How to Play

You are trapped between a dream and reality. The room you're in is real. Or it was.

  • Click objects to inspect them. Each interaction reveals a fragment of truth.
  • Your sanity drains over time. Low sanity causes the world to fracture.
  • Choices matter. Other players' choices echo around you as ghost data.
  • Loops happen when you take the wrong exit. The room resets — but you remember.
  • Hints are limited based on your plan. Use them wisely.

⚠ This game contains themes of unreality, psychological disorientation, and loop mechanics. Sanity is not guaranteed.
